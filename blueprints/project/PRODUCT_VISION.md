# Product Vision

> Describe what the product should become.

---

## Purpose

Define the functional vision of the project.

This document explains **what** the product should achieve and **why**.

It does **not** describe **how** it will be implemented.

---

## Use when

* Designing a new feature.
* Evaluating a functional change.
* Reviewing the product direction.
* Making product decisions.
